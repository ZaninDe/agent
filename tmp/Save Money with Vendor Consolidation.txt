Benefits of Vendor Consolidation:  
 
• Reduced Costs  - by centralizi ng vendor relationships & contract 
management, fewer managers need to spend their time with vendors. 
Additionally, strategic purchasing results in lower prices as economies of scale 
can be leveraged to negotiate favorable terms for competing vendors.  
 
• Increased Service & Support  - having a single partner to outsource your 
printing or consulting will result in deeper relationships and improved service 
levels, as the vendor will deem your account to be more strategic to them.  
 
• Improved Quality  - once you have  organized your purchasing and send your 
business to a single vendor, they will improve quality at the risk of losing your 
contract for the following year. Always expect & demand high quality.
