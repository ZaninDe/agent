Show Responsibility with a Sustainability Report  
 
Regulatory compliance, climate change awareness, and strong corporate governance 
have lead to a rapid increase in the number of organizations who choose to provide a 
Sustainability Report  to their internal and external stakeholders. Good corporate 
citizenry has many business benefits including cost savings, increased staff 
recruitment and retention, and improved  customer loyalty and brand preferences.

• Sustainability Reporting  - what is your Global Reporting Initiative Index?
